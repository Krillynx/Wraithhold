Mutations

The Murmuring Mist: Inhabitants may develop murmurclaws—shadowy, ephemeral talons that flicker and fade—or wraithskin, which renders the wearer nearly invisible in the mists. Some even grow ethereal wings, allowing brief glides in shadows.


The Dreamblight: Fears given form, these mutations vary wildly. Victims might wear a nightmare carapace of twisted patterns, or sprout blightfangs dripping a hallucinogenic venom that distorts the minds of others.


Phantasmal Parasites: These hosts may grow a phantom limb or spectral eyes, allowing them to pierce through dimensions both physical and spiritual. Haunted veins twist with ethereal tendrils, defensive yet uncontrollable.


The Crystal Plague: Victims develop crystalline growths that glimmer like armor, often forming shardspikes along limbs or gemstone eyes capable of refracting light. Though protective, each mutation pulls the bearer closer to a silent, frozen existence.


The Abyssal Corruption: Twisted, abyssal tendrils emerge, darkness radiates from void-like eyes, and a shadowed carapace absorbs light, granting stealth at the price of a gradual unraveling of self.


The Luminous Rot: Bioluminescent scales form along the infected's skin, casting an eerie glow that attracts both friend and foe. Glowspore growths pulse with dangerous beauty, a warning of their deadly effects.
