Locations

The Obsidian Spire

At the heart of Wraithhold stands the Obsidian Spire, a dark monolith of cold, glassy stone that absorbs the city’s dim starlight, returning none of it. It looms, untouched by time or trespass, and radiates a silent promise—or warning—of hidden knowledge. Legends say it holds the answer to Wraithhold’s mystery, or perhaps the path to true escape, though none have passed its walls to know for certain.


The Crystal Gardens

Alive with shimmering crystals that grow like trees, the Crystal Gardens glow with an eerie, pale luminescence, illuminating spectral forms and fragments of ancient memories that drift between the vines. While beauty abounds, so does danger, for the crystals are guarded by a jealous sentinel who punishes those who would harvest the power of these glittering treasures.


The Grand Basilica of Silence

Within its towering columns and carved walls, the Basilica’s silence weighs like a heavy fog. Even the faintest noises are smothered to a whisper, and within its nave, one cannot help but feel like an intruder disturbing an ancient slumber. Some claim the Basilica holds a place beyond time, a liminal space where the boundaries between this world and the next blur.


The Eternal Market

Deserted stalls line this open-air square, their wares untouched yet somehow impossibly preserved. Ghostly merchants flicker in and out of view, offering items whose origins are as mysterious as their purposes. Those brave enough to barter their Essence or make an offering may leave with treasures or curses, for here, payment must always be made.


The Forgotten Amphitheater

Beneath a sky locked in eternal twilight, the seats of this crumbling amphitheater face a stage where memories are woven and tragedies replayed by spectral actors for an unseen crowd. To witness these performances is to glimpse slivers of the past, yet what is revealed may change the observer as much as the world they inhabit.


The Shattered Bridge

A lone imperfection in Wraithhold’s flawless visage, the Shattered Bridge reaches across a bottomless chasm, only to falter midway. Its remaining stones dangle precariously, suggesting that to fall from its heights is to vanish beyond even the pyres’ reach. The bridge’s whispers seem to call to the lost, its broken path a symbol of paths abandoned, or destinies shattered.
